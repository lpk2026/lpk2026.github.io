Four years.
Four years of hard work and endless labor,
of challenges faced and lessons learned.
Four years of late nights, early mornings, and moments that tested our determination, but also shaped our character.
Four years of overcoming hardships, pushing ourselves beyond limits, and discovering what we are truly capable of.
Four years of laughter, shared memories, and unwavering support from friends who became family—
not by blood, but by a bond stronger than any grade or class.
This is our journey at Lycée Pilote Kasserine: a journey of growth, resilience, and unity, marking the culmination of our baccalauréat and the beginning of everything that lies ahead.
